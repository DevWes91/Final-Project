My trip to Italy in 2017 was my first time exploring the Amalfi coast with friends. We stayed in Nerano, a tiny village near Sorrento along the Amalfi coast. We rented a boat and traveled up and down, stopping in Positano for a day trip. Another day we took a boat to the Island of Capri for the day where we stayed a beach club and swam, ate and drank wine. The beauty of the Mediterannean is unparalleled!
